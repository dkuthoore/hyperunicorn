## Background Information: HyperUnicorn

HyperUnicorn is a hypothetical DeFi protocol that enables users to trade perpetual-style exposure using Uniswap LP positions.

The protocol supports two types of activity:

* **Vault activity** — users deposit into a vault, which actively market makes and manages positions on their behalf  
* **Direct trading activity** — advanced users create and manage their own custom positions

For more info, please see [this](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jy9zEgn2gbAQrQvjd1EfbmxHx_4bjJKWZMf7fDGz4R4/edit?usp=sharing).

Your task is to design and build a mini points system that incentivizes meaningful activity across both types of users.

---

This assignment is intentionally open-ended. There is no single “correct” solution. We care most about how you think, make decisions, and communicate tradeoffs.

Please spend **no more than 4–6 hours** on this assignment.

Please prioritize clarity and decision-making over completeness. We prefer a smaller, well-thought-out solution over a large, overengineered one. We are looking for:

* strong judgment  
* clear thinking  
* thoughtful communication  
* ability to prioritize

## **What to Build**

### **1\. Points System Design**

Design a points system for HyperUnicorn.

The goal is to incentivize real, valuable activity on the protocol while discouraging low-quality farming and obvious gaming. Your system should be grounded in realistic user behavior on the protocol.

Your system should:

* reward meaningful participation across both vault users and direct traders  
* be reasonably simple to understand and implement  
* reflect thoughtful tradeoffs between fairness, simplicity, and resistance to abuse

---

### **2\. Mock Activity Data**

Create mock user activity data over time.

Use this data to demonstrate how your points system behaves across:

* different users  
* different activity patterns  
* multiple time periods

The system does **not** need to update in real time, but it should show how results evolve over time.

---

### **3\. Frontend Dashboard**

Build a frontend interface that presents the points system.

The interface should help users understand:

* their points  
* their relative standing  
* how their activity contributes to their score

---

### **4\. Written Explanation (README)**

Your README is a core part of this assignment.

Please **overcommunicate your thinking**. Clearly state any and all assumptions you made. We are explicitly looking for:

* how you approached the problem  
* what decisions you made  
* why you made those decisions  
* what tradeoffs you considered  
* what concerns or edge cases came to mind  
* what you intentionally simplified  
* what you would improve with more time

---

### **5\. Video Walkthrough**

Record a **\~3 minute screen recording** walking through:

* what you built  
* your points system  
* key decisions and tradeoffs  
* what you would improve next
